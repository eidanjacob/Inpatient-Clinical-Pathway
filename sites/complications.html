Post-Operative Complications

Perioperative

Up to a week after procedure.
  • Gastro-intestinal: ileus
  • Respiratory:
    • sore throat
    • atelectasis
    • prolonged intubation
    • pleural effusions
    • pneumothorax
    • pneumonia
  • Excessive bleeding (< 1500 cc)
  • UTIs, urinary retention
  • Technical: hardware failure, etc.
  • Neurologic:
    • thecal penetration
    • nerve root injury
    • spinal cord injury
  • SMA syndrome
  • Visceral injury

Early Post-Operative

Up to a month after procedure.
  • Wound Complications: hematoma, seroma, or dehiscence
  • Gastro-intestinal: ileus
  • Respiratory:
    • pleural effusions
    • pneumonia
  • UTIs

Late Post-Operative

Beyond one month after procedure.
  • Wound infection
  • Hardware failure
  • Curve progression
  • Bone fusion failure
