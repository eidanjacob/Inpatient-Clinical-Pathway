Activity/PT
  • Log roll q2 hours and PRN until patient can roll independently
  • Continue PT and OT daily
  • Continue ambulation TID
  • OOB to chair TID
  • Climb stairs
  • Use brace as needed, if ordered and available
  • SCDs while in bed and asleep
