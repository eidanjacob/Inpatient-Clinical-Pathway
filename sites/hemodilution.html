Hemodilution

  1. Calculate Acceptable Blood Loss
  2. Prepare RBC bags from blood bank x2 (ask anesthesia tech)
  3. Withdraw blood from arterial line as shown (either option A or B)
  4. Replace volume with fluid of choice
  5. Colloid 1:1 ratio, crystalloids 1:3 ratio
  6. Label, time, and store blood at anesthesia station
